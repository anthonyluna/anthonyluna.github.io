The Venue

Where are we headed to celebrate: Camp Arnaz is located in the lower Ojai Valley and inland from the city of Ventura. Camp Arnaz is named after Actor Desi Arnaz who previously owned the property, which was purchased by the Girl Scouts of CA Central Coast in 1975. Camp Arnaz has recently been designated as a Monarch Breeding and Research Institute with pollinator gardens. It is also home to a variety of wildlife, including red-eared turtles, herons, fish, squirrels, owls, blue jays, swallows, woodpeckers, red tailed hawks and more! Location and Directions: Camp Arnaz 155 Sulphur Mountain Rd, Ventura, CA 93001 Note: once you are on Highway 33, it is a sudden and slight right-hand turn so keep your eyes peeled.
